League of Legends

League of legends is a MOBA (Mulitplayer Online Battle Arena) devolped by Riot Games. The game will start with 5 players on each side in a designated role and the goal is to destroy the other teams base. Top, jungle, Mid, ADC and Support

Roles & Lanes

Each Lanes in the Rift provides the team with different positions to play while in a match. Each Lane includes different Roles and Positions that the players may choose from to allow for each players playstyle.

Lanes

Top Lane is at the top of the Rift ad is usually filled with roles such as tanks and bruisers. The Mid Lane is in the center of the Rift and is often occupied by mages and assassins. The Bottom Lane is at the bottom of the Rift and typically features a duo of an Attack Damage Carry (ADC) and a Support. The Jungle is the area between the lanes, where players known as Junglers roam to secure objectives and assist their teammates.

Generating Gold

Generating gold is done by defeating neutral mosnters, destroying objectives, and defeating other players in on the map.
